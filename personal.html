Personal Interests

This page documents all my interests that do not fit into research or professional interests.

Books

This post gives a rough idea of the kind of books I am interested in. Currently, I am hugely into the works of Jim Butcher (The Dresden Files, Codex Alera, The Cinder Spires), Max Gladstone (The Craft Sequence), Brandon Sanderson (The Cosmere Sequence, including Mistborn, Warbreaker and the Stormlight Archive). I am also interested in the works of Trudi Canavan (Black Magician Trilogy, Age of the Five, Millenium's Rule), Rick Riordan (Percy Jackson and associated works), Jonathan Stroud (the Bartimaeus trilogy, Lockwood & Co), John Scalzi (Old Man's War).

I was also a major fan of the Star Wars Expanded Universe (now known as Star Wars: Legends). Grand Admiral Thrawn is my favourite Star Wars character, Timothy Zahn is my favourite Star Wars writer and Luke Skywalker+Mara Jade is my OTP. As far as I'm concerned, if Mara no longer exists in the newfangled Disney canon, then Disney canon does not exist for me.

My favourite series of all time is The Wheel of Time, by Robert Jordan, though my favourite author of all time is probably Isaac Asimov. The question of which is my favourite book is much more difficult to answer so I'm not even going to attempt to do it; however, Good Omens: The Nice and Accurate Prophecies of Agnes Nutter, Witch by Terry Pratchett and Neil Gaiman is probably up there somewhere on the list.

The books I have read, as well as my reviews can be seen on my Goodreads profile.

Movies

Largely speaking, I do not watch movies. When I was younger, 10 Things I Hate About You and Jaane Tu Ya Jaane Na were my favourite movies, and I only really watched either adaptations of speculative fiction or rom-coms. Now, I recognize that my high regard for those movies is because I hadn't really explored cinema much, and I really can't say what my favourite movies are. My favourite MCU movie is Guardians of the Galaxy.

Games

Growing up, except for the short time I owned a PlayStation 2, the only videogames I used to play were real-time strategy games. My favourite videogame ever is probably Age of Mythology, and I was always in love with Age of Empires sequence most of my life (it was, quite literally, the first videogame I played). I stopped playing videogames around the same time I retired my PlayStation (that is, around 2008).

I love playing cards; in particular, I like rummy, 29 and hearts. In general, I prefer trick-taking games, though I have never played bridge.

I have recently gotten into boardgames. Some classics I enjoy include Cluedo, Stratego, Risk and Scrabble. My favourite game, currently, is Dominion; I play Dominion Online with the handles cbg_iitk and anuragsahay - I have all the available sets to play until December, 2016, so if you'd like to play with me, do let me know. I love Duration cards, which is why Adventures and Seasides are my favourite expansions of Dominion.

Music

When I was younger, I largely listened only to rock and alternate rock. I have since expanded my tastes to country, progressive rock and pop. 3 Rounds and a Sound by Blind Pilot is my favourite alt rock song. The Coke Studio (Pakistan) rendition of Tajdar-e-Haram by Atif Aslam is my favourite song in a Hindustani language. Hey Jude is my favourite song by the Beatles.

My current most listened to song is Heathens by twenty one pilots (for the Suicide Squad album).

History and Mythology

History and mythology are my first and second love (most likely because of the fact that they are the respective subjects of the games I played most in my childhood, namely Age of Empires and Age of Mythology. I'll not go into much detail here at present (I might fill it out later), but I love the storytelling aspects of history and myth, which is what eventually got me into reading books in the first places.
