Deutsche Bank (DB Center)

 

Summer Analyst, May 2015 — July 2015

I was a summer intern at the DB Centre office in Mumbai, and worked in the regulatory impact team. I designed an intra-net based tool using Django (a Python-based web-development framework) which calculates the exposure faced by the bank against listed derivatives from a client portfolio uploaded as a .csv file. The exposure is calculated using the Current Exposure Method under the ambit of the European Union implementation of the Basel Accords, specifically Basel III. The tool also calculated the RWA (risk-weighted assets) held by the bank against the portfolio, and the corresponding Tier I regulatory capital to be held by the bank, and was meant to be used by Senior Management to make client pricing decisions to adequately compensate DB for the capital held aside.

I got a full-time offer for my work here, but I ended up not joining.
