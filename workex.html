Work Experience

Boston Consulting Group

 

Associate, September 2016 — September 2017 I was an Associate at the Gurgaon office of BCG India for one year. During this time, I worked on a few government cases, one internal case, and a few investments.

Deutsche Bank (DB Center)

 

Summer Analyst, May 2015 — July 2015 I was a summer intern at the DB Centre office in Mumbai, and worked in the regulatory impact team. I designed an intra-net based tool using Django (a Python-based web-development framework) which calculates the exposure faced by the bank against listed derivatives from a client portfolio. The exposure is calculated using the Current Exposure Method under the ambit of the European Union implementation of the Basel Accords. The tool also calculated the risk-weighted assets held by the bank against the portfolio, and the corresponding Tier I regulatory capital to be held, to be used by Senior Management to make client pricing decisions to adequately compensate DB for the capital held aside. I got a full-time offer for my work here, but ended up not joining.
