Students’ Senate

I was associated with the Students’ Senate at IIT Kanpur for virtually my entire stay there. I served in the following capacities:

  • Three terms as Senator for my (UG Y11) batch (2011-12, 2013-14, 2014-15)

  • Three terms as Students’ Senate nominee to the Senate Undergraduate Committee (2012-13, 2013-14, 2014-15) of the Institute

  • Students’ Senate nominee to the Senate Students’ Affairs Committee (2014-15) and the Senate Education Policy Committee (2014-15) of the Institute

  • Secretary, Students’ Senate (2012-13)

  • Parliamentarian, Students’ Senate (2013-14)

  • Chairperson, Students’ Senate (2014-15)

I was also a member, convener, or chairperson of several ad-hoc and standing committees of the Students’ Senate, and the student member in various ad-hoc bodies constituted by the Institute, particularly committees of the (academic) Senate.

The archives of my time as Chairperson are here. If you want a specific document related to my time in the Senate, or would like to discuss some aspect of student or academic governance at IIT Kanpur, please feel free to contact me.

When I was Chairperson, Prof. A.R. Harish was the Chief Counsellor, Students’ Gymkhana and Prof. Indranil Manna was the Patron, Students’ Gymkhana. I was succeeded by Shubham Atreja, and my fellow officebearers were Abhimanyu Arora (President, Students’ Gymkhana), Shivendu Bhushan (General Secretary, Science and Technology), Chirag Agrawal (General Secretary, Games and Sports), Chetan Garg (General Secretary, Films and Media) and Shaleen Mandowara (General Secretary, Cultural). I was a Senator in the Senates which were chaired by Aditya Gupta (2011-12), V. Srinivisan (2013-14) and myself (2014-15); I served as Parliamentarian in Srini's time. I was Secretary to the Senate chaired by Ankit Bhutani (2012-13).

Prof. A.R. Harish was the Chairman, SSAC when I was a member of the SSAC, and Prof. Indranil Manna was the Chairman, Senate when I was a special invitee to the Senate.

During my time on the SUGC, I served under the Chairmanships of Prof. Arbind Lal (2012-13), Prof. Pankaj Wahi/Prof. Y.N. Singh (2013-14) and Prof. D.P. Mishra (2014-15). My personal favourite time on the SUGC was 2012-13, when the other student members of the SUGC were Aditya Gupta, Ankit Bhutani and Ankur Pandey; there was an abundance of people whose names started with A on the committee that particular year (including the Chairman and the senior-most member of the secretarial staff). I also think that Ankit, Ankur and I are probably the only trio of students who have served on the SUGC together for two consecutive years (2011-2013).
